TOESHEE / COOKIE POLICY

How we use cookies.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Toeshee uses them, and the choices you have regarding their use.

LAST UPDATED: JUNE 12, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or other internet-connected device) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website operators information about how the site is being used.

Cookies may be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third parties, such as analytics and advertising providers ("third-party cookies").

2. Who We Are

This Cookie Policy applies to the website operated by TOESHEE (also referred to as "we," "us," or "our"). If you have any questions about how we use cookies, please contact us at compliance@center-source.com.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies on our website:

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, security, and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without these cookies, and they cannot be switched off.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
toeshee_cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preferences so we don't show the consent banner on every page visit 1 year First-party
toeshee_lang Stores your preferred language (English or Spanish) for the website interface 1 year First-party

3.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They allow us to measure and improve the performance of our site.

Cookie Name Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users by assigning a randomly generated number as a client identifier 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Persists session state across page requests 2 years
_gid Google Analytics Distinguishes users and stores information about how visitors use the website 24 hours
_gat Google Analytics Used to throttle request rate 1 minute

3.3 Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable the website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, more personal features. These cookies are not used to track you across other websites.

3.4 Marketing & Targeting Cookies

We currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies on our website. If this changes in the future, we will update this Cookie Policy and seek your consent where required by law.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your cookie consent preferences
  • Remember your language preference
  • Understand how visitors navigate and use our website
  • Monitor and improve the performance and reliability of our website
  • Ensure our website functions correctly across different browsers and devices

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are placed by third parties. We do not control these cookies and they are subject to the relevant third party's privacy and cookie policies. Third parties whose cookies may be present on our website include:

  • Google Analytics — for website performance measurement. See Google's Privacy Policy and opt-out tool.
  • Google Fonts — for loading web fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Cookie Choices

You have several options for controlling or limiting how cookies are used on our website:

6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner at the bottom-left of the screen. You can:

  • Accept All — Allow all cookies described in this policy
  • Decline — Only strictly necessary cookies will be used

Your choice is remembered for one year. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for our website.

6.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can configure your browser to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser
  • Alert you when a cookie is placed on your device

Please note that restricting cookies may impact your experience on our website. For guidance on managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6.3 Opt Out of Analytics

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use the browser's localStorage API to store your consent preferences (toeshee_cookie_consent) and language preference (toeshee_lang). Unlike cookies, localStorage data is not transmitted to our servers; it remains on your device. You can clear this data through your browser's developer tools or by clearing your site data.

8. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites that you do not want your online activity tracked. As there is currently no industry-wide standard for DNT, our website does not respond differently to DNT signals. We encourage you to use the cookie controls described above to manage your preferences.

9. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If we make significant changes that affect your rights, we will notify you through a prominent notice on our website or, where we have your contact details, by email.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us:

  • Email: compliance@center-source.com
  • General enquiries: sales@toeshee.com

For more information about how we handle your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.
