What this solution covers.

Five operational capabilities delivered as a unified, SLA-driven service layer.

Technical Incident Triage

Rapid classification and prioritization of platform errors, outages, and degraded service events to minimize user impact and resolution time.

Transaction Failure Handling

Dedicated workflows for investigating failed, pending, or stuck transactions including gas fee analysis and mempool diagnostics.

Smart Contract Issue Support

Operational guidance for smart contract interaction failures, reverted transactions, and ABI mismatches across EVM-compatible chains.

L1–L3 Escalation Support

Tiered escalation models ensuring frontline agents triage effectively and complex technical cases reach engineering with complete incident context.

Engineering Coordination

Structured handoff processes between support operations and engineering teams for systemic issues, ensuring accountability and faster resolution.
