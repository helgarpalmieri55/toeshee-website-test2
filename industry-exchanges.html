What we deliver for crypto exchanges.

KYC / AML Support

High-throughput identity review and AML support for regulated exchange environments. Our teams help manage verification queues, document review, sanctions screening, and enhanced due diligence.

Account Access Issues

Support for login issues, account recovery, 2FA problems, locked accounts, and user verification concerns. We resolve access friction while protecting platform security.

Transaction & Withdrawal Support

Operational support for deposit delays, withdrawal reviews, failed transactions, and user concerns around fund movement. Our teams manage sensitive cases with structured escalation discipline.

Exchange Operations

End-to-end CX operations for centralized exchanges, including queue management, user education, incident response, multilingual coverage, and quality-controlled execution.

Security Escalations

Structured handling for fraud signals, suspicious activity, compromised accounts, and high-risk user cases. We help route critical issues quickly without creating operational gaps.

Market Expansion Support

Multilingual and multi-market support for exchanges entering new regions. Our teams adapt to local user needs, regulatory expectations, and platform-specific support flows.

From day one to long-term operational excellence.

Platform Immersion & Team Setup
